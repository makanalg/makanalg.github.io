## Adding to the tool belt 
You can't really go wrong with having extra tools in your tool belt. It allows you to have the resources you need to solve a variety of problems. In the programming world, design patterns are an important tool to add to that tool belt. So, what is design patterns anyway? Well, to put it into simpler terms, design patterns are solutions to recurring problems. They are "templates" or guidelines on how to solve specific problems. They are NOT, however, solutions to *all* your problems. They can be great if used correctly and under the right circumstance, but it should not be forced as it could just cause more problems and unecessary stress.
